Academic Information Portal

About the CICT Grade Portal

A central platform for grades, schedules, records, and academic standing for the BSIT program at ISUFST Dingle Campus.

College of ICT — Dingle Campus
{# ── Mission / Purpose Section ── #}

Empowering Academic Transparency

The CICT Grade Portal is designed to give students, faculty, and administrators a single place to review academic records, monitor progress, and manage core academic workflows for the BSIT program.

By centralizing grades, subject loads, schedules, and profile information, the portal aims to make key academic data easier to access so that students and faculty can respond early to grade or enrollment risks.

The portal serves the College of Information and Communications Technology (CICT) at the Iloilo State University of Fisheries Science and Technology, Dingle Campus.

Data Privacy & Security

All student and faculty data is handled with strict confidentiality. The portal uses Supabase-backed authentication with role-based access, ensuring that academic records are only visible to authorized users.

New account registrations require admin approval before access is granted, and account activities are tracked via an audit trail for transparency and accountability.

Open Access for Students

Students can review their grades, subject loads, class schedules, and profile information at any time. The portal is accessible on mobile and desktop devices as a progressive web app.

Retention Policy Awareness

Public informational pages help applicants, current students, faculty, and parents understand BSIT program requirements, probation rules, and re-enrollment processes before signing in.

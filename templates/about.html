Academic Information Portal

A central portal for grades, schedules, records, and academic standing.

The CICT Grade Portal gives students, faculty, and administrators a single place to review academic records, monitor progress, and manage core academic workflows for the BSIT program.

What the portal supports

  • Student access to grades, subject loads, schedules, and profile information.
  • Faculty workflows for class records, grading, and student monitoring.
  • Administrative oversight for users, subjects, settings, and audit trails.

Retention standards

BSIT students are expected to maintain academic improvement, protect a semester GWA of 3.0, and avoid repeated failures in major and general education courses.

Institutional Standards

Academic Integrity & Standards

{# Policy sections #}
{# Section 1 #}
1

Academic Performance

BSIT students must demonstrate consistent academic improvement. Failure to satisfy these standards may result in academic probation or programmatic transfer.

{# Sub-items as "Ledger" cards #}

Course Failures

Failing three or more Major or General Education courses in one semester may lead to transfer advisement.

GWA Requirement

Standard: 3.00 or better per semester.

{# Section 2 #}
2

Academic Probation

A warning period designed to provide students with the necessary support and guidance to improve their academic standing.

Trigger Action/Duration
GWA < 3.0 Probation for the succeeding semester
First-time Freshmen Error Probation instead of immediate transfer
On-Probation Violation Recommended for Dismissal/Transfer
{# Section 3 & 4 #}
3

Academic Dismissal

Violation of academic policies while on probation leads to a recommendation for dismissal or enrollment in other programs.

4

Re-enrollment (LOA)

Students returning from Leave of Absence must satisfy all return prerequisites as evaluated by the Program Chair.
